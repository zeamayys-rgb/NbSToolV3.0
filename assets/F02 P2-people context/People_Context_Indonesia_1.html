Economy i

The average net income of individuals in West Kalimantan is Rp 2.94 M. The predominant sector is Agriculture (Rp 2.31 M), followed by Services (Rp 3.18 M) and Industry (Rp 3.52 M). In 2024, GDP at current prices was Rp 246.8 T, GDP per capita Rp 44.6 M, growth 4.9%, with PPP Rp 11,420, Gini 31.8% and CPI 106.4.

Health i

There are 312 health facilities in Kapuas Hulu, with 1,840 medical staff and 196 doctors. Stunting among under-5s is 24.5%, wasting 7.2%, underweight 16.1%, overweight 3.4%. The three most common diseases are acute respiratory infection, hypertension and pulmonary TB.

Housing & Human Settlements i

Households with safe drinking water in West Kalimantan: piped 34%, protected well 28%, refill/bottled 22%, rainwater 16%. The three most common sources are refill water, borehole/pump well and piped water (32%, 27%, 24%). Most household wastewater goes direct to river.
