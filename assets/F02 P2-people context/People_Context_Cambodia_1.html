Social (Demography)i

The selected area is located in Siem Reap Province, which has a population of 1.09 M people, with 238,000 households and an average household size of 4.6 persons. The province's population density is 104 per sq km, with a population growth rate of 1.3% per year and a gender ratio of 96 males per 100 females. In 2024, 9,800 immigrants were recorded in the province.

Employmenti

At the national level — no provincial breakdown is available — the unemployment rate of Cambodia is 0.7% and the labour force participation rate is 69.8% as of 2024. Nationally the majority of people work as skilled agricultural workers, service & sales workers, and craft & trade workers, with employment trends showing a gradual increase over 2020–2024.

Housing & Human Settlementsi

In 2024, the housing landscape in Siem Reap Province consisted of 58% wooden stilt houses, 27% brick/concrete houses, and 15% mixed/other structures. In the province, 76% of households have access to an improved drinking water source while 71% use improved sanitation/toilet facilities.
