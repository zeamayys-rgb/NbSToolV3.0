Economyi

At the national level, the GDP per capita of Lao PDR in 2024 was USD 2,080, with a GDP growth rate of approximately 4.0%. The top contributing industries are agriculture, mining & energy, and services. The national GDP distribution by sector shows 30% agriculture, 32% industry, and 38% services, with a national Gini ratio of 38.8%. Land use in Luang Prabang Province comprises 96,000 ha private agricultural land, 142,000 ha non-private agricultural land, and the remainder across other categories.

Healthi

There are 62 health facilities in Luang Prabang, including 4 public and 2 private hospitals, and the most common diseases are respiratory infections, diarrhoeal diseases, and dengue. At the national level, the number of medical workers and doctors in Lao PDR is 22,400 and 3,100 respectively, the prevalence of stunting among children under 5 is approximately 30.2%, and the percentage of fully vaccinated infants reached 74.6% in 2024.
