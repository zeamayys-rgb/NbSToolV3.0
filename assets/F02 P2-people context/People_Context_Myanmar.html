Educationi

In Hpa-An Township, 286,000 people have completed schooling and the literacy rate is 89.7%. The township's educational infrastructure includes 512 schools, served by 7,400 teachers, with 118,000 students enrolled as of 2024. At the state level, the literacy gender gap in Kayin is 92.1% of males versus 87.4% of females (4.7 percentage points).

Healthi

There are 74 health facilities in Hpa-An Township, with a healthcare workforce of 1,920 medical workers (doctors, nurses, midwives), of whom 240 are doctors, and a disability prevalence of 4.6%. At the state level, Kayin has 6 public and 3 private hospitals, a stunting prevalence among children under 5 of 28.9%, the most common diseases being malaria, respiratory infections, and diarrhoeal diseases, and infant immunization coverage of 76.8% in 2024.

Housing & Human Settlementsi

In 2024, the housing landscape in Hpa-An Township consisted of 34% permanent, 44% semi-permanent, and 22% non-permanent dwellings, occupied by 421,000 people. The three primary drinking water sources are tube well / borehole, piped water, and protected dug well; 68% of households have access to safe drinking water, wastewater is managed mainly through on-site septic systems, and 59% of households use improved toilet facilities. At the state level, housing production and demand in Kayin is trending up, clean water supply production is 42,000 m³ per day, and community-based total sanitation covers 64% of villages.
