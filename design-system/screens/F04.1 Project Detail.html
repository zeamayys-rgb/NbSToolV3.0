Privacy level

Data Sharing Terms & Conditions

1. Scope of data shared

When you raise your privacy level, SCeNe Coalition may share project metadata, baseline summaries, ecosystem descriptions, and aggregated carbon estimates with verified members, funders, and the public — depending on the level selected.

2. Irreversibility

Lowering your privacy level after data has been viewed or shared may not fully retract that data from third parties who have already accessed it. Once shared publicly (Level 3), summaries may be cached or indexed externally.

3. Collaborator notification

All project collaborators are automatically notified by email when the privacy level changes. The change is also logged in the project History for audit purposes.

4. Sensitive information

Personal contact details, financial data, and GPS coordinates at sub-hectare resolution are never shared publicly regardless of privacy level. Only aggregated spatial footprints are published.

5. Your rights

You retain ownership of your project data at all times. You may request data deletion or correction by contacting the SCeNe Coalition data team. Requests are processed within 30 business days.
