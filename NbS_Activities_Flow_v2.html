Data note. This visual is built directly from the ELIGIBLE activities-longform sheet (Activities Deep Dive) without altering source values. The single Trajectory & Pathway column is split into three diagram levels — 2014 land cover (Forest / Non-Forest), trajectory class (C1/C2 … C6, with its meaning), and intervention pathway (Protect / Manage / Restore). In the Benefit level, the source codes tag each benefit as General or Unique across (N)ature, (P)eople, (C)limate; the redundant General benefits are collapsed into one node per category, while Unique benefits keep their individual names. Two rows marked "N/A — no specific activity defined for this ecosystem" are omitted. Whitespace in text was trimmed so identical labels merge. No values are invented.
